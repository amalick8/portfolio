Impact

Awards programming concentrates traffic into short, high-stakes windows. These analyses translate messy logs and engagement streams into stable trends: load health, referral mix, device splits, and anomalies worth escalating before show night.

Architecture

Notebooks: reproducible cells from extract through summary stats and visuals, documented for non-engineering readers where it matters.

Data wrangling: pandas-centric transforms with clear naming and intermediate tables for auditability.

Outputs: charts and tables tuned for stakeholder decks and internal review—not one-off screenshots.

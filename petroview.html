Case study

PetroView

Full-stack energy intelligence: oil-market signals, regime and volatility context, and decision-grade dashboards built on real series and statistical models.

Next.js TypeScript Tailwind FastAPI Python pandas scikit-learn FRED · OWID

Product preview

Live UI screenshots from the repo, cross-fading on a loop. Replace with assets/petroview-demo.gif (or a video) whenever you have a capture.

What it does

PetroView connects global oil signals into a single operational view: structure, risk, and volatility alongside forecasts and narrative context. It is built as a reproducible stack with clear data lineage from ingestion through reports.

Highlights

  • Time series ingestion, normalization, and validation.
  • Supply, demand, and pricing signal analysis.
  • Multi-model forecasting (naive, regression, ARIMA, ensembles).
  • Regime and volatility tracking for live dashboards.
  • Interactive dashboards and exportable research outputs.

Architecture

Frontend: Next.js (App Router), TypeScript, Tailwind, Recharts. Backend: FastAPI, SQLModel, pandas, statsmodels, scikit-learn. Data sources include FRED and OWID energy series.
